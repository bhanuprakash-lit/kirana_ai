## 2. Scope and the roles we play

We process personal data in two capacities:

- **As a Data Controller / Data Fiduciary** — when we decide why and how data is
  processed (e.g. our website visitors, our direct account holders, job
  applicants, marketing contacts).
- **As a Data Processor / Data Processor on behalf of our customers** — when a
  business using our products uploads data about *their* own customers (for
  example, a shopkeeper recording a customer's credit/"udhaar" details in Outlet
  AI). In that case the business is the controller and we process that data
  under our agreement with them. See the Outlet AI Privacy Policy for detail.

This policy is written primarily for [COUNTRY — e.g. India] under the **Digital
Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)** and the **Information Technology
Act, 2000** and rules made thereunder. Where we serve users in other regions, we
also seek to honour the **EU/UK GDPR** and the **California Consumer Privacy Act
(CCPA/CPRA)**; the additional regional rights are described in Section 11.
